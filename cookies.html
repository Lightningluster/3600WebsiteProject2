Cookie Policy

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help remember your preferences, login details, and site activity. Some cookies are essential for the site to function properly, while others help us improve performance and content.

Managing Cookies

You may choose to disable cookies through your browser settings. However, please note that certain features of the website may not work correctly if cookies are disabled. For more details on managing cookies, visit All About Cookies.

  • Essential cookies: Required for site functionality
  • Performance cookies: Help track and improve performance
  • Preference cookies: Save user settings
